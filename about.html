Mission

Overview of Church Ministries' Achievements For over a decade, Church Ministries has been dedicated to serving the community. Since 2018, we have been offering monthly food item donations worth Rs 1000 to five families and visually impaired individuals. Additionally, we have invested Rs 50,000 in church refurbishments. Our most recent renovation took place in the Arcot area in 2022.

Vision

Our mission is to construct 1000 churches, thereby enriching and strengthening various communities. We also strive to extend our assistance to over 100 visually impaired and differently-abled individuals on a monthly basis. Our ministry is devoted to supporting widows and their children by providing educational aid.
